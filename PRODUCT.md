# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, engineering peers, and collaborators who want to quickly understand Zhang Xinxin's backend engineering depth and AI Agent direction.

## Product Purpose

This personal site presents a seven-year backend engineer transitioning into AI Agent application development. It should make the visitor remember both the person and the technical trajectory: Golang backend systems, high-concurrency infrastructure, LangGraph/MCP Agent engineering, and a playful working style.

## Brand Personality

Handwritten, restless, capable.

## Anti-references

Avoid polished SaaS templates, glassmorphism, purple-blue AI gradients, identical card grids, corporate resume pages, and overly cute childish decoration. The site should feel like a programmer's crayon notebook, not a kindergarten worksheet or a portfolio template.

## Design Principles

- Show technical credibility through concrete systems, tools, and project notes.
- Keep the page imperfect on purpose: tilted paper, loose strokes, crossed-out edits, and marker highlights should carry the identity.
- Put the human voice next to the engineering details.
- Make the first viewport immediately recognizable as Zhang Xinxin's personal notebook.
- Treat motion as tactile paper and pen movement, with reduced-motion support.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for readable text. Do not depend on color alone for meaning. Respect reduced-motion preferences by disabling jitter, page-motion, and decorative animation where requested.
